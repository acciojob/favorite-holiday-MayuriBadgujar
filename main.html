Dwarka

Dwarkadhish temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna.The temple is located at Dwarka, Gujarat, which is one of the Hindu pilgrimage sites known as Chardham.Located on the shore of the river Gomti, this city is described in legends as the capital of Krishna.

  1. Bet dwarka -Located around 30 km from the main town of Dwarka Beyt Dwarka is a small island
  2. Dwarka Beach -Dwarka Beach is a good place to relax in the evenings.
  3. Dwarka Lighthouse - Tourists can enjoy the panoramic view of the sunset.
  4. Sudama Setu -. The bridge was named after Sudhama, Lord Krishna's childhood friend.
  5. Nageshwar Jyotirlinga -One of the 12 famous self-existent temples, Nageshwara Jyotirlinga Temple too houses a curious myth about its origin. The giant, beautiful and artistic statue of Lord Shiva mesmerizes tourists and pilgrims alike with its aesthetic appeal.
  • Chhappan Bhog -Chappan Bhog is a special prasad consisting of 56 food items that are offered to Lord Krishna on Krishna Janmashtami.
  • Peda -Peda is one of the most popular offerings to Lord Krishna. Made of fresh mawa, milk, sugar, ghee and cardamom powder, pedas are slightly brown in colour and best consumed when fresh and soft.
  • Khaman Dhokla - khaman dhokla is famous dish of Gujrat
  • Khichdi Khichdi is a simple, classic Indian dish made with rice and yellow mung lentils. It is actually a very healthy recipe
  • Balushahi - Balushahi recipe is a traditional sweet originating from India.
